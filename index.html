What is El Tranque de Miami?

El Tranque de Miami emerged from the 2018 Nicaraguan protests, taking its name from the roadblocks ("tranques") that symbolized citizen resistance and the vital Nicaraguan diaspora community in Miami. We publish a monthly bulletin that tracks judicial capture, democratic backsliding, and the actions of the diaspora in defense of human rights and political freedoms.
