Judiciary Watch

We monitor Nicaragua’s judiciary to document political appointments, legal reforms, and patterns of institutional capture that undermine separation of powers. Our bulletin highlights key cases, shifts within the Supreme Court and other courts, and the legal tools being used to entrench authoritarianism.

Diaspora Organization

Based in Miami, we organize members of the Nicaraguan diaspora around shared goals: protecting human rights, defending political prisoners, and building sustained pressure for democratic restoration. This includes public events, strategy sessions, and coordination with other diaspora hubs.

International Advocacy

We engage with international organizations, human rights groups, and policymakers to ensure Nicaragua’s democratic crisis remains visible. Through statements, briefings, and coordinated campaigns, we amplify the voices of those inside Nicaragua who face direct repression.

Research & Documentation

Over time, we aim to produce concise reports, timelines, and visual materials that document institutional changes, human rights violations, and the role of the diaspora. These resources are designed to be useful for journalists, advocates, and researchers.
