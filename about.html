Origins

El Tranque de Miami emerged from the 2018 Nicaraguan protests, taking its name from the roadblocks ("tranques") that symbolized citizen resistance and the vital Nicaraguan diaspora community in Miami.

Mission

Our mission is to advocate for democratic restoration in Nicaragua through informed analysis, diaspora organization, and international awareness campaigns that highlight human rights and political freedoms.

We track and analyze systemic challenges to Nicaragua's judiciary, documenting political appointments and advocating for constitutional reforms to restore separation of powers.

Why a Monthly Bulletin?

Nicaragua’s political crisis is long-term, structural, and often underreported. The bulletin exists to give the diaspora and international allies a regular, evidence-based snapshot of how institutions are being captured, where resistance is emerging, and how people abroad can contribute strategically.
